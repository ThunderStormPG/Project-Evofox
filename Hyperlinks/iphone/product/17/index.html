Overview

The new iPhone 17 delivers essential innovation with an all-new design, the powerful **A18 Bionic chip**, and an enhanced Dual-Camera system. It features a bright and vibrant Super Retina XDR display, incredible battery life, and superior durability, making it the perfect upgrade for everyday life.

Key Features

Performance: The **A18 Bionic chip** provides smooth and efficient performance, handling demanding games and apps with ease while maximizing battery efficiency.

Camera System: Capture stunning photos with the advanced **Dual-Camera System**: a 48MP Main camera and a 12MP Ultra Wide. This system features Smart HDR and improved low-light performance for beautiful results every time.

Display: The **6.1-inch** Super Retina XDR display is sharp and vivid, delivering incredible contrast and color accuracy for all your content.

Durability: Built with an aerospace-grade aluminum frame and the ultra-tough **Ceramic Shield** front cover, the iPhone 17 is built to last. It maintains an IP68 rating for water and dust resistance.
